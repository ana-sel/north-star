# Compass — the practice model (the daily-load backbone)

> Resolves the central confusion: too many parallel daily demands (paths +
> qualities + foundations + habits) stacking into overwhelm. This is the backbone
> every screen (Today, Paths, Foundations, Log·Habits) must obey. Governed by
> [`spec-00-principles.md`](spec-00-principles.md) principle 11 (the Middle Way).

---

## 1. The one idea: **effort separates a Practice from a Habit**

| | **Practice** (the theme) | **Habit / Ritual** |
|---|---|---|
| Effort | High — conscious, deliberate | Almost none — automatic |
| How many at once | **ONE** (the theme of the day) | Many (they're cheap) |
| Lifespan | Temporary — until embodied | Lasting |
| On Today | the **Primary**, expandable | a light checklist |
| Lives in | a Path or a Foundation | Log · Habits (rituals) |

Deliberate practice works best **spaced and interleaved**, not massed (Ericsson;
Cepeda 2006; Rohrer & Taylor); habits form through **frequent, stable-context**
repetition (Lally 2010; Fogg). Those are *two different rhythms* — so Compass runs
**two engines**.

## 2. Two engines, two rhythms

**A · Rituals (habits) — DAILY & automatic.**
Small, low-effort anchors in a stable morning/evening context. Frequency = **daily**,
because that's what builds automaticity; missing the odd day is fine. Many coexist
(they're cheap). This is the **Foundations baseline** + any habits you add. Lives in
**Log · Habits**.

**B · The practice pool (qualities) — ROTATED, a few times a week.**
You hold a small **active pool** (2–4 qualities you're growing). Each *day* you
practise **one** — your **theme of the day**, chosen or rotated. Across a week each
quality gets ~2–4 sessions. **This is deliberate: spacing + variety integrate a
quality *better* than daily grind — and keep it from getting boring** (§2a). Qualities
are also practised **situationally** ("practise patience *when* it's triggered"). The
pool lives in Paths / Identity; today's one theme surfaces on Today.

> You do **not** grind one thing for 6–8 weeks. You rotate a small pool, one per day,
> and the pool evolves as things embody.

## 2a. Frequency & dosage — the science (the "exactly how much / how often")

| Type | How often | Why (science) |
|---|---|---|
| **Ritual habit** (foundation or added) | **Daily**, small, same time/place | Automaticity needs frequency + a stable cue (Lally); odd misses are fine |
| **Quality / practice** | **~2–4×/week**, one per day rotated, **+ situationally** | Spacing (Cepeda) + interleaving (Rohrer) beat daily massed for retention; variety sustains motivation (SDT) |
| **Building a *new* foundation** | Near-daily at first → then settles into the ritual | Automate first, then maintain on autopilot |

Integration is **distributed** — a quality embodies after enough *spread-out* reps
(roughly a couple of dozen focused sessions over ~2–3 months, plus situational
practice), self-claimed (spec-you §0.1). Never a 6–8-week block of one thing.

### The rotation gap — no quality two days running
Because qualities are *spaced*, the day's theme **rotates with a gap**: a given
quality is **never offered two days in a row** — there's always at least a day
between reps. Even on 3–4 paths, *yesterday's practice doesn't reappear today.*
(Habits are the opposite — daily.) This is the spacing effect, made mechanical: the
app suggests the theme from whatever's been rested longest, always overridable.

## 2b. Build gradually — one habit at a time (the Fabulous model)

Never drop a routine on someone. *(Fogg — Tiny Habits: start tiny, celebrate;
Fabulous's proven onboarding; progressive overload.)*
- **Onboarding starts with ONE tiny habit** — e.g. a glass of water on waking. Nothing else.
- **The next habit is added only once the current one is steady** — one at a time.
- **Each habit starts at its smallest dose and grows:** no-screens 15 → 30 → 45 → 60
  min; walk 5 → 10 → 20; sit 1 → 3 → 5; frequency 3×/wk → 5× → daily. The escalation
  lives inside the foundation/path that owns the habit.
- **Paths add their habits gradually too** — not the whole bundle at once.
- Principle: **slow is durable.** A routine assembled one anchor at a time *stays*;
  a routine dumped all at once collapses.

## 3. What a day asks (the whole load)
1. **Your rituals** — the daily light habits (Foundations baseline + added). Automatic, collapsible.
2. **One theme** — a single quality from your pool, effortful, expandable (the if-when intention). **You choose which one today**; it's never yesterday's quality (the gap, §2a).
3. A few **plan tasks**.

So a day is: a light routine you barely think about **+ one thing you actually lean
into + your errands.** Variety day to day; focus within a day.

## 4. Foundations, Paths, and adding your own

- **Foundations = the baseline.** The must-have morning/evening rituals every adult
  deserves — a minimum level, always-on, mostly automatic (wake + light, a grounding
  breath, name-a-feeling, one-line journal…). This is the **floor routine**, and the
  main thing to get *perfect*.
- **Paths = optional bundles.** Starting a path adds **its habit(s) → your rituals**
  and **its quality(ies) → your practice pool.** Removing a path removes them.
- **Add your own, no path needed.** You can add a **habit** (→ rituals) or a
  **quality** (→ pool) directly. It's **logged identically** — acquisition is by
  *repetition*, and repetition is repetition whatever its source.
- **The theme** (today's one quality) can come from a Foundation, a Path, or a
  standalone quality. Foundations can be the theme (they're the floor → priority).

## 5. Where everything lives — the "what is where" map

| Concept | Edit / lives here | Surfaces on Today as | Read-only elsewhere |

| **The theme** (1 active practice) | Paths / Foundations | the **Primary** (expandable) | — |
| **Rituals** (morning/evening habits) | **Log · Habits** | a gentle pointer + the rest-rhythm | — |
| **Paths** (journeys) | Paths | only the theme surfaces | Progress (momentum), Identity (mirror) |
| **Foundations** | Foundations area | if it *is* the theme | — |
| **Qualities** | Identity | via the theme | Paths |
| **Tasks** | Plan | "Your day" (a few) | — |
| **Sleep / mood** | Log · Sleep | Arrival | Progress |
| **Earned virtues** | Identity | — | Paths (Earned shelf) |

**One home per concept** — this kills the page repetition. The grounding breath, for
example, lives *once* (in Regulate); it's referenced elsewhere, never duplicated.

## 6. Interactions this implies

- **Done / Not today** on the theme — with an *optional, pressure-free* reason
  (too tired · forgot · too hard · didn't feel right). Over time the pattern
  surfaces gently in **Discover** (*"you tend to skip on low-sleep days — want to
  make it smaller?"* — Fogg: shrink the behaviour). Never interrogating, never shame
  (principles §1.5).
- **Completed items collapse** — Today stays calm as the day is done (Taoist emptying).
- **Only the theme is expandable** (full if-when practice); rituals are simple checks.
- **Skips feed reduction, not judgement** — repeated skips → "make it tinier," not
  "you failed."

## 7. Fixes this mandates (concrete)
1. **Naming collision:** the earned virtue **"Foundation"** collides with the
   **Foundations** area → rename the virtue (→ *Constancy*). Foundations that aren't
   completed never appear in the **Earned** shelf.
2. **Today** realigns to *one theme + light rituals* (not 3 co-equal cues).
3. **Rest rhythm** cards adopt the standard card language (visual consistency).
4. **Log · Habits** is the single home of rituals; embodied practices graduate into it.

## 8. Resolved
- **Rhythm:** habits = daily; qualities = ~2–4×/week, **rotated from a small pool,
  one theme per day** (you choose today's). Not one thing for 6–8 weeks (§2a).
- **Paths = bundles** (habits → rituals, qualities → pool); **standalone adds** work
  the same, logged identically.
- **Foundations = the baseline morning/evening rituals** (spec-foundations §1b).
