Step 5 of 5 · important
Privacy & AI — be clear-eyed
🔒 What stays on your device
Your name, timezone, and raw sleep times are stored locally and in your private Compass account. They are never sold or shared with advertisers.
🤖 What the AI sees
When you ask for coaching or insights, Compass sends anonymised pattern data — durations, scores, habit counts — to an AI model (currently OpenAI). Your name and exact times are never sent. The AI provider processes this under their own privacy policy. You cannot control what they do with their logs.
