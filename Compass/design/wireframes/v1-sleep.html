Bryan Johnson (Blueprint) reference

Target: 22:00–06:00 · 8h. Variance ≤30 min every day including weekends. No screens 1h before bed. Cool room ~18°C. No food 3h before sleep. Consistent schedule is more protective than perfect duration.

The app defaults to 23:00–07:00 — a gentler starting point. The note can reference circadian science once 14+ days of data exist.

Pattern detection logic

  • 1 night ≥30 min late — no nudge (single data point = noise)
  • 2 nights ≥30 min late — gentle note appears
  • 3+ nights ≥1h late — suggest intermediate stepping-stone target
  • 7+ days consistently off — offer to reconsider the target itself
  • Trend improving — quiet acknowledgement only, no fanfare
