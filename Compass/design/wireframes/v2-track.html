V2 — Capture & Browse

The navigation shape that survives all the way to V4: 4 mode-tabs (Today · Log · Map · You) plus a floating Chat pill on every screen. Structure by mode of engagement, not by feature.

New · 4-tab architecture New · Floating chat pill Moved · Sleep entry → Log Merged · Mood & energy into Sleep · 1–10 score Redesigned · Body inputs + ✕ exclude + BMI slider Redesigned · Target uses the same dial as entry New · Today = needs you + tasks done/not New · Money (Quiet-Capital port) New · You → Trends with W/M/Y/A

4 mode-tabs

☾ TodayGlance — needs-you + tasks done/not + quick log.
◈ LogCapture — Sleep (with mood/energy) · Habits · Body · Money.
◇ MapDirection — Year/Map placeholder in V2; Month kanban active.
◯ YouBrowse — Trends · History · Money detail · Settings.

Body redesign

  • ✕ toggle per field — untouched fields write NULL, not 0
  • BMI auto from profiles.height_cm + weight, with status
  • Tension → renamed Body feel (relaxed/easy/tight/sore) + optional body-area chip
  • Flag → renamed Today I noticed — optional chips
  • Calories + macros grouped in one section

Money — Quiet Capital in Compass language

  • 8 categories with QC's palette renamed Roof / Move / Food / Essentials / Living / Investing / Giving / Aspire
  • Quick log in Log → Money; full app in You → Money
  • Calm beige adaptation of QC's dark zinc design
  • CSV importer for existing QC data

Floating Chat (V2 shell)

  • Bottom-right pill, ChatGPT-style
  • Always visible across every screen
  • V2 ships shell with disabled composer
  • V3 wakes it up: Mission / Goal / Free modes, contextual

Try it

  • Tap Log, switch sub-tabs (Sleep dial, Body ✕-toggle, Money quick)
  • Tap Map, swipe the horizontal kanban
  • Tap You, open Trends or Money
  • Tap the 💬 pill any time
