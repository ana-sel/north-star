V3 — Direction & Conversation

Same 4-tab shape. Now quality practice lives on Today, goals live on Map with a thoughtful add-flow, the Now → Mission map shows where you're really pulling, and the chat pill wakes up with three contextual modes.

Live · Today's quality card New · Map Year + Add-goal flow New · Now → Mission viz New · Chat: Mission / Goal / Free New · You → Identity

Quality on Today

  • One active quality per day with a one-line cue
  • Single ☐ check — no scoring, no streak shame
  • Auto-rotates from your max 3 active qualities
  • Schema: qualities, quality_logs

Add a goal — 4 steps

  • 1. Name + pillar — anchors goal to a colour
  • 2. Want vs have-to — joy capacity is built from wants only
  • 3. Mission filter — at least one truth in three checkboxes
  • 4. Qualities + skills — Have / Missing / Unknown badges, never guilt

Now → Mission map

  • Pillar-coloured paths from Now to Mission
  • Dashed grey path = have-tos (passport)
  • ⚠ glyph between conflicting goals (£100k ↔ water trip)
  • CTA → "What blocks happiness?" surfaces tensions

Chat modes (contextual)

MissionPulls from your stated mission + values. Suggests refinements.
GoalActive when looking at a specific goal. Helps break it down.
FreeOpen thinking. No agenda.

Try it

  • Tap the ☐ on the quality card
  • Open Map → Year, tap ＋ Add a goal to walk the 4-step flow
  • Open Map → Map to see Now → Mission
  • Tap the 💬 pill — Mission mode opens with a real suggestion
  • Open You → Identity
