Mobile nav caps at 5 slots. In V4, Trends & History stop being top-level tabs — each tracker now carries its own trend + history inside Track, freeing the two slots for the cross-cutting Chat and World. This matches the final structure in v4-world.md.

Energy as atmosphere

The world shows state, never numbers. 4 invisible energy types (Physical · Mental · Emotional · Spiritual) read every tracker and translate into sky, light, fog, and road condition across the 7 regions.

The Cartographer — hard rules

  • Past tense, third-person, max 2 sentences, never "you"
  • No scene on day 1 — earned, not given
  • Max one scene per 48 hours
  • No cross-pillar scene without both streams logged
  • Unmapped regions stay visibly empty
  • Patterns fade if the data stops
