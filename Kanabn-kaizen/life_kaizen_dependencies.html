Why the old map felt wrong

It tried to show mission, dependency, categories, outcomes, wishes, and tasks all in one visual. Financial freedom hijacked the whole tree.

What this fixes

Each view answers one question. Financial freedom is one pillar, not the parent of everything. Dependencies are a separate overlay.

Daily use

You mostly live in Chat + Today. The big views are for planning, monthly reviews, and when you feel lost.

Card types

Every card has a type: ★ Outcome, Project, Task, ⟲ Habit, Practice, State, Wish, or Maint. They sit at different levels, not mixed together.

1. Compass

This is the why layer. Calm and small, not a giant board. The app's north star.

Why

🎯 Mission

I am a happy person who is living with purpose and meaning. My mission is to live a happy life.

I am an explorer and learner of this world's rules, systems, and invisible energies — how things interact, what life is made of, and what gives it meaning.

Through this understanding, I share knowledge with people who want to receive it, so they can gain clarity and turn their pain, confusion, and dreams into desired outcomes using clear, almost algorithmic action plans and systems.

I build my money freedom empire so I feel safe to create value for people, live in a Vastu house or wherever I want, and live without the pressure of needing a job while living with meaning and purpose.

For now, I choose solitude to heal my past pain, release shame and guilt from wrong choices, refine myself, and measure my life only by what feels resonant and attractive to me.

Values

Freedom, clarity, solitude, self-refinement, meaning, contribution.

Current season

Healing. Build stability, health, money freedom, and app foundation. Choose solitude. Measure by resonance.

Mission filter

Does this support happiness, freedom, clarity, self-refinement, solitude, and meaning? If not → archive or Someday.

3. Goal Tree

Zoom into one selected goal at a time. This prevents the whole life from becoming visual spaghetti.

How to break it down

Rule

A goal tree answers: "What pieces make this goal real?" Only zoom into one goal or project at a time.

Not for everything

Do not show every life category here. Use the Pillars view for the full picture.

Kanban rules

  • If a card moves between columns 3 times without progress → review: split, redefine, or move to Someday.
  • Max 3 cards in "In progress" at once. Congestion is not progress.
  • Today is a separate view — it pulls from "In progress" + "Planned."
  • Habits (meditation, sleep, NVC) live here as recurring — they don't get "Done."
