OPERATING RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. MONTHLY REVIEW (1st Sunday of month)
   - Open Life Map board → read Compass (mission card at top)
   - Scan all 6 lists: do my Execution priorities still serve these?
   - Move 8-10 cards from Backlog → This Quarter

2. WEEKLY REVIEW (Sunday evening or Monday morning)
   - Pull 3-5 cards from This Quarter → This Week
   - Check In Progress: anything stuck > 2 weeks? Split, redefine, or return to Backlog

3. DAILY
   - Pick 1-3 cards from This Week → In Progress (if under WIP limit)
   - Do the work
   - Move to Done when finished

4. ⟲ RECURRING CARDS
   - Habits and Practices stay in In Progress permanently
   - Use Trello checklist inside the card for daily/weekly tracking
   - Example: meditation card → checklist with days of the week

5. DEPENDENCY AWARENESS
   - Before pulling a card, check its description for "→ after: X"
   - If the prerequisite isn't Done yet, don't pull this card

6. WIP LIMITS
   - In Progress: max 3 non-recurring cards (habits don't count)
   - This Week: max 5
   - This Quarter: max 10

7. STUCK CARD RULE
   - If a card bounces between columns 3 times → split it, redefine it, or move to Backlog with a note why
